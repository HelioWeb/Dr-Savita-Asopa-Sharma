About

Dr Savita Sharma Qualified as the “ Best Outstanding Student “ in 2007 from University of Rajasthan, has always been a first class honors student collecting awards throughout her academic career for each year.

Pursued her post graduate MFDS from the prestigious Royal College of surgeons of Edinburgh. Not only believes in learning through latest dental conferences and courses but also in giving back as a keen academician and has taught as a clinical lecturer at Newcastle University of dental sciences and is proud of currently teaching at the Kings dental college London, ranked first in the world for dentistry.

Has been a part of my dentist since 2012, providing high end cosmetic and general dentistry involving Smile Designs via composite bonding and teeth straightening by INVISALIGN Go Also performs Enlighten and zoom teeth whitening, denture excellence ( private and flexible dentures) , tooth colored fillings crowns and veneers.
